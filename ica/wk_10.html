42 Dugg

Detroit rapper 42 Dugg found his calling during an especially dark time in jail. While in solitary confinement, he began writing rhymes to keep his grip on sanity, and once free, was inspired to pursue music full-time. Within a few years, he signed with 's label and brought his ruthless street style to a series of mixtapes including 2019's Young and Turnt and 2021's Free Dem Boyz. In 2022 he teamed up with roster-mate on the album Last Ones Left. He also appeared on the high-profile compilation Gangsta Art and landed a one-off hit, "U-Digg," with and Veeze. In 2024, he released his first official full-length album, 4eva Us Neva Them.
